Company Employment Policies

Eligibility Criteria for All Positions:
- Must be legally authorized to work in the country
- Pass background check and reference verification
- Meet minimum age requirement (18 years)
- Possess required education credentials
- Demonstrate relevant skills and experience

Remote Work Policy:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must maintain reliable internet connection and workspace
- Required to attend in-person meetings as scheduled
- Performance metrics must be maintained regardless of location

Benefits Package:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) retirement plan with company matching
- Paid time off (PTO) starting at 15 days annually
- Professional development budget ($2,000 per year)
- Flexible working hours (core hours 10 AM - 3 PM)

Performance Review Process:
- Quarterly check-ins with direct supervisor
- Annual comprehensive performance review
- Goal setting and career development planning
- Promotion considerations based on performance and availability

Code of Conduct:
- Maintain professional behavior at all times
- Respect diversity and inclusion principles
- Protect confidential company information
- Report any ethical concerns to HR department
