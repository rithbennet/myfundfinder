Software Engineer Hiring Criteria

Technical Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field
- 3-5 years of professional software development experience
- Proficiency in at least two programming languages (Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, etc.)
- Experience with databases (SQL and NoSQL)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)
- Understanding of software development lifecycle and agile methodologies
- Experience with version control systems (Git)

Soft Skills:
- Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking abilities
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- Ability to work independently and in team environments
- Adaptability to new technologies and learning mindset
- Attention to detail and quality-focused approach

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in Computer Science or related field
- Experience with microservices architecture
- Knowledge of DevOps practices and CI/CD pipelines
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Industry certifications (AWS, Azure, etc.)

Interview Process:
1. Initial screening call (30 minutes)
2. Technical coding assessment (1-2 hours)
3. System design interview (1 hour)
4. Behavioral interview (45 minutes)
5. Final interview with hiring manager (30 minutes)

The ideal candidate should demonstrate both technical excellence and cultural fit within our organization.
